== Installation ==
**Note:**  WP Legal Cases is  now built on the [Piklist](http://piklist.com) framework.  Piklist should be installed from the plugins director before installing WP Legal Cases.

= METHOD 1 (Preferred) =
**Install Piklist**
1. Log in to your WordPress site.
1. Go to Plugins -> Add New.
1. In the search box, type "Piklist".
1. Click "Install".
1. Once the installation is complete, click "Activate Plugin".

**Install WP Legal Cases**
1. Return to Plugins -> Add New.
1. In the search box type "WP Legal Cases".
1. Cick "Install".
1. Once the installation is complete, click "Activate Plugin".

= Method 2 =
**Install Piklist**
1. [Download Piklist from the plugin directory](https://wordpress.org/plugins/piklist/).
1. Login to WordPress and go to Plugins-> Add New -> Upload Plugin.
1. Click the "Choose File" button and select piklist.zip from your hard drive.
1. Click "Install Now".
1. Once the plugin has been installed, click "Activate".

**Install WP Legal Cases**
1. Download WP Legal Cases from the plugin directory.
1. Login to WordPress and go to Plugins->Add New->Upload Plugin.
1. Click the "Choose File" button and select wp-legal-cases.zip from your hard drive.
1. Click "Install Now".
1. Once the plugin has been installed, click "Activate".
